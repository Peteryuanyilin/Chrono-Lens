# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: May 30, 2026*

Welcome to ChronoLens. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our services, we may collect the following types of information:

*   **Account Information**: If you sign up using Email or Google Sign-In, we collect your email address and basic profile information provided by the authentication service.
*   **Camera and Photos**: We require access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to scan and identify landmarks. Images may be processed securely via third-party AI services (e.g., Google Gemini).
*   **Location Data**: We may collect your device's GPS location and EXIF data from uploaded images to provide accurate landmark identification and mapping features (using Google Maps API).
*   **Usage and Analytics**: We use tools like Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to collect non-identifiable usage data, crash logs, and app performance metrics to help us improve the app.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
*   To provide, maintain, and improve ChronoLens.
*   To process and identify landmarks via AI APIs.
*   To synchronize your history and preferences across devices.
*   To monitor app performance and fix bugs.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted third-party services that assist us in operating our app, such as:
*   **Google Firebase**: For authentication, database hosting (Firestore), analytics, and crash reporting.
*   **Google Gemini API**: For AI-powered image analysis and identification.
*   **Google Maps API**: For location rendering and geographic context.

*These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.*

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data. You can manage your account settings within the app or contact us directly.

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
